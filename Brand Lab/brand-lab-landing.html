What you get

A live brand sheet that updates as you work.

  • Palette.

    Six colours, click to recolour, drag to reorder. Copy as hex, CSS or Canva.

  • Typography.

    Pair fonts from twenty pairings, or pick your own headings and body from Google Fonts.

  • Mockup.

    A real hero section with eyebrow, headline, subhead, button and three offerings. Click any text to edit it.

  • Contrast check.

    WCAG ratings for every text-on-background pair in your palette, so you know which combinations are safe for body copy and which are headings only.

Export the whole sheet as a PNG when you're done.

Who it's for

Built for brand strategists, web designers and virtual assistants who build websites and want a fast way to test combinations before opening Figma or starting a build.

Also useful for the people we work with. Some clients want to come in, pick what they like and hand it over. Others want to explore for themselves. The tool works for both.
