About Beth

Thirty years in.
Still curious.

[ portrait · Beth Alpert, LCSW ]

Beth Alpert has spent nearly 30 years working with children, families, educators and school communities. From Chicago to London, Brussels, Kuala Lumpur and now The Hague.

She has been a teacher, school counsellor, head of counselling and senior leader. She has fostered teenagers, spent a decade advocating for children in juvenile court, and raised two sons across four countries. Her work is grounded in clinical training, real world experience and a genuine belief that most people, given the right kind of support, are far more capable than they know.

She holds a Master of Social Work and Master of Education from Loyola University, is a Licensed Clinical Social Worker, and works with clients globally online.

Her approach is evidence based, deeply human and, when the moment calls for it, genuinely funny.
